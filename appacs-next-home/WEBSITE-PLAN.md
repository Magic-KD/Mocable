# APPACS Foreign Trade Website Plan

## Positioning

Recommended website focus: **APPACS as an OEM/ODM USB cable and fast charging cable manufacturer**.

The project brief contains one product conflict: the company sheet lists "Diesel generator sets, gas generator sets", while the product sheet, uploaded images, catalog direction and keyword file all point to USB-C, iPhone, 3-in-1 and fast charging cables. The homepage prototype follows the cable product direction.

## Homepage Structure

1. Hero: APPACS brand, factory-direct OEM/ODM cable positioning, quotation CTA and product image.
2. Strategy strip: states the recommended site focus for SEO, AIO/GEO and paid ads.
3. Product category entry: USB-C, iPhone, 3-in-1, LED display, watch charging and custom logo cables.
4. Priority products: the three uploaded U87 product images as the first product cards.
5. Product detail direction: repeatable detail-page content model for SEO and ads.
6. OEM/ODM capability: customization, sample support, packaging and project follow-up.
7. Cooperation flow: requirement, sample, logo/packaging, production/QC.
8. Factory trust blocks: factory proof, procurement proof and SEO proof.
9. Applications: importers, brand owners, promotional gifts and e-commerce sellers.
10. Market landing plan: Russia, Europe, America, South America, Middle East and Southeast Asia.
11. SEO/AIO architecture: priority keyword landing pages and blog/FAQ entries.
12. FAQ: concise answer blocks for buyers and AI answer engines.
13. Resource CTA: catalog, product comparison and procurement FAQ.
14. Inquiry form: name, email, country and product requirement.

## SEO And AIO Direction

Primary SEO route:

- `/usb-c-cable-manufacturer/`
- `/fast-charging-cable-manufacturer/`
- `/custom-usb-cable-manufacturer/`
- `/usb-c-to-lightning-cable-manufacturer/`
- `/240w-usb-c-cable-supplier/`

Market route:

- `/markets/usb-cable-supplier-uae/`
- `/markets/usb-cable-supplier-saudi-arabia/`
- `/markets/usb-cable-supplier-nigeria/`
- `/markets/usb-cable-supplier-south-africa/`
- `/markets/usb-cable-supplier-indonesia/`

AIO/GEO route:

- Short answer FAQ pages for OEM, MOQ, certifications, private label, custom packaging and supplier comparison.
- Each answer should link back to a product or inquiry page.
- Avoid unsupported claims, fake customers and unverified certifications.

## Paid Traffic Direction

For future Google/Facebook/LinkedIn/TikTok ads, build landing pages around one product and one buyer intent:

- Custom USB Cable Manufacturer: private label and packaging ads.
- 3-in-1 Charging Cable Wholesale: distributor and promotional gift ads.
- LED Display USB-C Cable: product-feature ads.
- Country pages: importer/distributor traffic by region.

Each ad landing page should include product visual, buyer pain point, customization options, MOQ/sample CTA, inquiry form and WhatsApp.
